【GL-Advcanced】Suggesting a solution - 提出一个解决方案

发表于 2020-04-28 更新于 2020-04-29 分类于 EF ， GL ， Advanced

Vocabularies of the course

  1. alternative [ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv] adj.可供替代的; 非传统的; 另类的 n.可供选择的事物;
  2. alternatively [ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪvli] adv.(引出第二种选择或可能的建议) 要不，或者;
  3. Lexical [ˈleksɪkl] adj.词汇的;
  4. Making suggestions:
  5. What say we 我们怎么说
  6. What if we were to … 如果我们要
  7. Suppose we… 假设我们
  8. Let’s say we… 假设我们
  9. Giving alternatives:
  10. rather than/we could… 而不是…，我们可以; 与其…，不如…;是……而不是……；与其……不如……
  11. alternatively, we could.. 或者，我们可以…
  12. we could…instead 我们还是…
  13. Iceberg [ˈaɪsbɜːɡ] n.冰山(浮在海上的巨大冰块);
  14. Gourmet [ˈɡʊəmeɪ] n.美食家; 讲究饮食的人; 美酒美食品尝家;
  15. Connoisseur [ˌkɒnəˈsɜː(r)] n.鉴赏家; 鉴定家; 行家
  16. turn off the heat 关闭暖气;
  17. infinitive [ɪnˈfɪnətɪv] n. (动词的)不定式(英语中的动词不定式可单独使用，如she can swim中的swim，或带to，如she likes to swim) ;
  18. would rather…than.. rather than 与would 连用时;构成“would rather…than…”句式,意思是“宁愿……而不愿……”,表示主观愿望,即在两者之中选择其一.例如：
  19. She’d rather die than lose the children. 她宁愿死也不愿失去孩子们
  20. rather than 不与would连用时,表示客观事实,意为“是……而不是……；与其……不如……”.它连接的并列成分可以是名词、代词、形容词、介词(短语)、动名词、分句、不定式、动词等.现分述如下
  21. would rather意思是“宁愿、宁可、更、最好、还是为好”,后接动词原形,常省略为’d rather,表示优先选择的一种方式
  22. 其否定形式是would rather not do sth.would rather没有人称和数的变化,所有的人称一律用would rather.
  23. “would rather+动词原形”是英语中常见的一个惯用句式,美国英语中多用had rather.would (had)在此决无“过去”之意,它是一个情态助动词,且无词性、时态变化.
  24. Mr Li would rather not listen to rock music. 李先生不愿意听摇滚音乐.
  25. If you’d rather be alone, we’ll all leave here. 如果你宁愿独自呆着,那我们都离开这儿.
  26. He’d rather work in the countryside. 她宁可到农村去工作.
  27. You would rather stay at home and do some reading this evening. 今晚你最好呆在家中读点书.
  28. 二、如果在两者中进行取舍,表示“宁愿……而不愿……,与其……宁可……”的意思时,则可用would rather…than…或would…rather than…的句型

Content of the course

1. What do you suggest we do?

2. What can you and your classmates do to help one another learn English? Make some suggestions.Now give some alternative suggestions.

3. Did you use any of these phrases when giving suggestions and alternatives? What else might you say?

  • What if we were to …(+do) 如果我们要…
  • Suppose we … (+do) 假设我们…
  • Let’s say we … 假设我们…
  • Rather than…(+doing), we could…(+do) 而不是…，我们可以; 与其…，不如…
  • Alternatively, we could… 或者，我们可以…
  • Let’s … instead. 我们还是…

    4. Use the phrases we discussed to give suggestions for your next vacation.

  • Let’s say we spend a week in Hawaii.
  • Suppose we went to Fiji instead?
  • go to the mountains - go to the beach
  • eat gourmet food - see a movie
  • visit friends - spend time alone

    5. You and your family are discussing ways to save money.

  • Student A: Begin the discussion by suggesting one thing you can do to save money.
  • Student B: Give an alternative suggestion.
  • Example:
  • What if we were to turn off the heat and wear sweaters?
  • That’s good idea, but I really hate the cold. Alternatively, we could …

    6. Today we…

  • gave suggestions for learning English.
  • gave alternative suggestions.
  • suggested ways to save money.

    7. Before you go…

  • What do you think we should do about the environment? What are some alternatives to what we are doing now?
  • What could a foreigner do to get used to living in your country? Give some alternative suggestions as well.
  • Done
