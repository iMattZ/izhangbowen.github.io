Content of the course

1.Talk in pairs about your country’s national identity. What’s similar about people in your country? What are their differences?

2. Talk in pairs about how these factors define your country’s national identity.

  • language
  • music
  • dance
  • food
  • sport

3. Complete the survey to describe similarities and differences among people in your country.

  • Statements about national identity
    • 1.We all say hello in the same way.
    • 2.we all like the same sport.
    • 3.We don’t all celebrate the same holidays.
    • 4.We have a shared love of spicy foods.
    • 5.We don’t all like the same music.

Add a statement to describe a similarity or a difference that defines your country’s national identity.

4. Use the cards your teacher gives you to talk about national identity.

  • Student A: Use these factors to ask questions about the national identity of Student B’s country - language, music, sport, food, dance.
  • Student B: Answer Student A.
  • Swap roles and repeat.
