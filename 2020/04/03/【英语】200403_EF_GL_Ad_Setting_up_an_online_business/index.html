【GL-Advcanced】Setting up an online business - 开设在线业务

发表于 2020-04-03 更新于 2020-04-14 分类于 EF ， GL ， Advanced

您在想开一个网店。谈谈您喜欢您最爱的网站的原因，然后计划设计一下自己的网站会是怎样子的。
You’re thinking of starting an online business.
Talk about why you like your favorite websites, then plan what your own site could look like.

Vocabularies of the course

  1. free delivery 免费邮递 -selling points
  2. product range 产品范围; 产品名称; 产品类别; 产品种类; 产品系列
  3. user friendliness [ˈfrendlinəs] 用户友好性; 用户友好; 友善性; 用户友爱性; 用户友善性
  4. loyalty [ˈlɔɪəlti] n.忠诚; 忠实; 忠心耿耿; 要忠于…的强烈感情
  5. rationale(s) [ˌræʃəˈnɑːl] n.基本原理; 根本原因
  6. presence [ˈprezns] n.在场; 出席; 存在; 出现; (派遣的) 一个队; (尤指执行任务的) 部队;
  7. web presence Web展示
  8. user-friendly [ˌjuːzəˈfrendli] adj.方便非专业用户的; 便于使用的; 易于掌握的
  9. e-commerce websites [iː ˈkɒmɜːs ˈwɛbˌsaɪts] 电子商务网站;
  10. communication tools 沟通工具; 交流工具; 传播工具; 通讯工具; 通信工具;
  11. the landing page 登岸页; 着陆页; 登陆页面; 页面
  12. the online chat 在线聊天
  13. after sale service 售后服务
  14. refund and return policy 退换货政策
  15. selling points
  16. user loyalty 用户忠诚度
  17. rationale n.基本原理; 根本原因;
  18. publications [ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃənz] n.(书刊等的) 出版，发行; 出版物; 发表; 刊登; 公布;
  19. online support and consultation 在线支持和咨询
  20. competing advantages 竞争优势;
  21. profitable [ˈprɒfɪtəbl] adj.有利润的; 赢利的; 有益的; 有好处的
  22. fast delivery 快速配送
  23. product preference 产品偏好

Content of the course

  1. What do you do for work? What is your favorite website? Why do you like it?

  2. What are the most important elements of a website from the user’s perspective?

    • the landing page 登岸页; 着陆页; 登陆页面; 页面
    • colorful mages
    • online chat
    • preference/filters/searching history
    • user friendly
    • technical support

  3. Did you consider any of these elements of a website?
    What other things could be helpful to users?

    • convenience
    • product range
    • user friendliness
    • customer support
      Which do you think influences user loyalty the most? Rank the elements.

  4. Listen, and answer these questions.

  • 1.What is Debbie and Pierre’s business?
  • 2.Is it successful? What makes you think this?
    Listen again, and answer thses questions.
    • 1.What was the rationale behind creating this website?
    • 2.How did the couple choose their products?
    • 3.What can you do if you have technical problems?
    • 4.What can you do if you have questions about the products?
    • 5.What happens after you purchase your products? How long does it take?
    • 6.How have they improved their web presence?

  1. You and your partner are going to set up an online business.Decide what kind of business it will be and discuss the elements of your website.

    • Student A: Tell Student B(your colleague) what you think is the most important factor when designing your site.
    • Student B: Tell Studnet A whether you agree, and offer an additional factor to consider.
      Example:
    • I think we should make our site extremely convenient for users.
    • I agree. We also need to ensure that we have a wide range of products.

  2. Today we …

    • talked about why we like our favorite websites.
    • discussed some important elements in user-friendly sites.
    • created a plan for our own website.

  3. Before you go…

    • It takes most people just seconds to decide if they like a website. What do you think happens in those few seconds?
    • No website is perfect. What are some things you wish your favorite website could do?
