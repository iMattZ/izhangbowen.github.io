【GL-Advcanced】Explaining the rules - 解释规则

发表于 2020-04-17 更新于 2020-04-16 分类于 EF ， GL ， Advanced

Vocabularies of the course

  1. lenient [ˈliːniənt] adj.(惩罚或执法时) 宽大的，宽容的，仁慈的;
  2. strict [strɪkt] adj. 严格的(指必须恪守); 要求严格的; 严厉的; 恪守教规(或信条等)的;
  3. mandatory [ˈmændətəri] adj.强制的; 法定的; 义务的; n.受托者;
  4. Attendance is mandatory. 务必参加
  5. optional [ˈɒpʃənl] adj.可选择的; 选修的
  6. follow regulations 遵守规定
  7. come to an understanding 达成谅解；达成妥协;
  8. I must come to an understanding with her.我必须和她取得谅解.
  9. tolerate [ˈtɒləreɪt] v. 容许，允许(不同意或不喜欢的事物); 忍受; 容忍; 包容; (对药物) 有耐受性; 能经受(困难条件);
  10. She can no longer tolerate the position that she’s in.她再也受不了自己的处境了
  11. will be punished 会被惩罚
  12. punish [ˈpʌnɪʃ] v.处罚; 惩罚; 对…判罪; 判定…的处罚方式; 责怪(自己); 自责;
  13. be encouraged to 鼓励
  14. indicate [ˈɪndɪkeɪt] v.表明; 显示; 象征; 暗示; 间接提及; 示意;
  15. boligation [ˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃn] n.义务; 职责; 责任; (已承诺的或法律等规定的) 义务;
  16. should make efforts to 应该努力
  17. proper dress 正装
  18. continual [kənˈtɪnjuəl] adj.(令人厌烦地) 多次重复的，频繁的; 接连不断的; 连续的; 频频的;
  19. Lateness n.迟，晚
  20. canteen [kænˈtiːn] n.食堂; 餐厅; (士兵、旅游者等用的) 水壶; (装有一套刀、叉和勺的) 餐具盒

Content of the course

1. Is your boss lenient or strict?

2. What are the rules at your job or school? Which rules are mandatory? Which are optional?

3. Did you use any of these phrases when discussing the rules? What other phrases could you use?

  • follow regulations
  • come to an understanding
  • will not tolerate
  • will be punished
  • be encouraged to
    Which phrases indicate boligation? Which indicate the ability to choose?

4. Look at the list of rules. Which rules are mandatory? Which are optional?

    1. Everyone should make efforts to improve their English skills.
    1. Following the regulations regarding proper dress is obligatory.
    1. Employees must accept extra work if their manager requires it.
    1. Employees and managers may come to an understanding regarding sick leave.
    1. The company will not tolerate continual lateness.
    1. An employee must respond to their manager’s emails within one hour.
    1. Employees are encouraged to participate in social events.
    1. Any discussion of salaries will be punished.

5. You are giving a new employee orientation.

  • Student A: Tell Student B one of the rules at the office.
  • Student B: Ask Student A for details about the rule.
  • Example:
    • Employees must arrive by 8 in the morning.
    • What if I have to take my daughter to school?
    • Lateness will not be tolerated.

6. Today we…

  • discussed the rules at work or at school.
  • talked about mandatory and optional rules.
  • gave a new colleague an orientation.

7. Before you go…

  • If your were the boss, would you be lenient or strict? What rules would you enforce?
  • Parents sometimes have very strict rules. Which rules are mandatory in your family? Which are optional?
