【GL-Advcanced】Solving social issues - 解决社会问题

发表于 2020-04-05 更新于 2020-04-14 分类于 EF ， GL ， Advanced

Vocabularies of the course

  1. exist [ɪɡˈzɪst] v.存在; 实际上有; (尤指在困境或贫困中) 生活，生存;
  2. social problems 社会问题
  3. drug(s) [drʌɡz] n.毒品; 药; 药物 v.使服麻醉药; 用药麻醉; 使服兴奋剂; 在(食物或饮料中)投放麻醉药，下麻醉药
  4. peer pressure [pɪə(r) ˈpreʃə(r)] n. 同侪压力; 同辈压力
  5. unemployment [ˌʌnɪmˈplɔɪmənt] n.失业; 失业人数; 无业; 没有工作
  6. bad economy [bæd ɪˈkɒnəmi] 经济不景气
  7. anti-pollution [ˈænti pəˈluːʃn] adj.反污染的; n.防止污染；防止公害；防污染作用
  8. anti-pollution standards 防污染标准
  9. motivate [ˈməʊtɪveɪt] v.成为…的动机; 是…的原因; 推动…甘愿苦干; 激励; 激发; (就所说的话) 给出理由; 说明…的原因;
  10. strict [strɪkt] adj.严格的(指必须恪守); 要求严格的; 严厉的; 恪守教规(或信条等)的
  11. punishment [ˈpʌnɪʃmənt] n.惩罚; 处罚; 刑罚; 粗暴对待; 虐待;
  12. strict punishment 严惩
  13. gang [ɡæŋ] v.使结成一帮; 成群; 结伙; （一群人）联合起来（多为了威慑别人） ; 合伙攻击; 将（电气设备或机器）连接; 编组; （同轴） 联接; 接合; 使成套排列;
  14. gang [ɡæŋ] n.一帮，一伙(罪犯); 一群，一伙(闹事、斗殴的年轻人); 一伙(经常聚在一起的朋友);
  15. violence [ˈvaɪələns] n.暴力; 暴行; 狂热; 激情; 激烈的力量;
  16. gang violence 帮派暴力
  17. …

Content of the course

  1. What social problems exist in big cities?

  2. Describe a social problem in your country. Talk about the reasons for this problem.
    Which of the problems discussed is the most serious? Why?

  3. Did you use any of these words to describe social problems?

    • drugs
    • peer pressure
    • unemployment
    • bad economy

Group the words into problems and reasons for the problems.

  • Problems
    • drugs
    • unemployment
  • Reasons for the problems
    • peer pressure
    • bad economy

What other words can you add to the lists?

  1. What’s your opinion of these possible solutions for some of the problems?
    Compare your answers with those of your classmates.

    • I agree strongly / I agree / I disagree / I disagree strongly
    • 1.The government needs to set anti-pollution standards.
    • 2.Develop programs to motivate young people to stay in school.
    • 3.There needs to be strict punishment for gang violence.
    • 4.Give tax credits to private industry to create jobs for our unemployed.
    • 5.Renovate abandoned buildings to provide shelter for our homeless.
      renovate: restore to good condition

  2. Your government has three proposals it wants to carry out. You and a friend go to a local council meeting to object to these proposals.

    • build a large factory
    • cut police numbers
    • close 10% of public schools
  • Student A: Choose one proposal. Explain why it is a bad idea and what social problem it would increase.
  • Student B: Suggest Studetn A’s position and offer a different suggestion for solving the problem Student A’s talking about.
    Example:
    • Building a large factory would increase pollution and cause more health problems for our citizens.
    • Perhaps you could build a green factory that wouldn’t pollute the environment.
      council: a body of people responsible for administration of the city.
