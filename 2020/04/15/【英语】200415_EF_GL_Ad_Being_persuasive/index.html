【GL-Advcanced】Being persuasive - 做个具有说服力的人

发表于 2020-04-15 更新于 2020-04-16 分类于 EF ， GL ， Advanced

What’s the biggest environmental problem facing the world today? See if you can bring your classmates around to your way of thinking.
当今世界面临最大的环境问题是什么？看看您是否能够说服您的同学们同意您的观点。

Vocabularies of the course

  1. carbon emissions 二氧化碳排放量; 碳排放; 二氧化碳排放; 碳排放量;
  2. carbon [ˈkɑːbən] n.碳; 复写纸
  3. emission(s) [iˈmɪʃnz] n.(光、热、气等的) 发出，射出，排放; 排放物; 散发物;
  4. deforestation [ˌdiːˌfɒrɪˈsteɪʃn] n.毁林; 滥伐森林; 烧林
  5. sustainable living 可持续生活; 可持续生存; 永续生活
  6. sustainable [səˈsteɪnəbl] adj.(对自然资源和能源的利用) 不破坏生态平衡的，合理利用的; 可持续的;
  7. renewable energy 可再生能源
  8. renewable [rɪˈnjuːəbl] adj.可更新的; 可再生的; 可恢复的; 可延长有效期的; 可展期的; 可续订的;
  9. energy [ˈenədʒi] n. 精力; 活力; 干劲; 力量; 能源;
  10. persuade [pəˈsweɪd] v.劝说; 说服; 使信服; 使相信
  11. Given.. prep. 考虑到; 鉴于;
  12. It’s little wonder… 这没什么好奇怪的
  13. People realize … 人们意识到
  14. It goes without saying that… 不言而喻；毫无疑问；不言自明的；不用说；不消说。
  15. We own it to.. 我们拥有它
  16. crucial [ˈkruːʃl] adj.至关重要的; 关键性的;
  17. convince [kənˈvɪns] v.使确信; 使相信; 使信服; 说服，劝说(某人做某事);
  18. landfill(s) [ˈlændfɪlz] n. 废物填埋地(或场); 废物填埋; 填埋的废物;
  19. recycle [ˌriːˈsaɪkl] v.回收利用; 再利用; 再次应用，重新使用(概念、方法、玩笑等);
  20. recyclable [ˌriːˈsaɪkləbl] adj.可回收利用的; 可再循环的

Content of the course

1. What is the single most important issue affecting the world today?

2. Name three problems with the environment, and suggest three solutions.

3. Did you think of these phrases? What other phrases can you add to the list?

  • problems
    • carbon emissions
    • deforestation
  • solutions
    • sustainable living
    • renewable energy
  • What is your country doing to help protect the environment?

    4. Listen to the speech on global warming. What phrases does the speaker use to persuade listeners to help protect the environment?

  • Given.. prep. 考虑到; 鉴于;
  • It’s little wonder…
  • People realize …
  • It goes without saying that…
  • We own it to …
  • We must…

5. What do you think is the most crucial thing we each can do to help protect the environment?

  • Student A: Try to convince Student B that your issue is the most essential.
  • Student B: Convince Student A that your issue is even more critical and must be dealt with first.
  • Example：
    • Given that our landfills are growing rapidly, we should focus first on reducing waste and recycling.
    • I agree that recycling is important, but it goes without saying that we must first focus on developing sources of renewable energy.
