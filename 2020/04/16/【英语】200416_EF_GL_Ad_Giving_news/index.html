【GL-Advcanced】Giving news - 通告新闻

发表于 2020-04-16 分类于 EF ， GL ， Advanced

Vocabularies of the course

  1. to break the news to sb 把这个消息告诉某人
  2. to break the bad news to sb 把坏消息告诉某人
  3. I’m sorry to have to tell you this. 很抱歉不得不告诉你这个
  4. I have some good news and some bad news.
  5. I’m going to tell this to you straight. 我要直截了当地告诉你
  6. I hate to be the one to break this to you.
  7. How did that happen? 怎么发生的; 怎么会这样
  8. How did that happen? Did you lose your keys? 怎么会呢？我的钥匙掉了吗？
  9. What am I gonna do? 我该怎么办
  10. What am I gonna do? I can’t get married. 我应该怎么做？我不能结婚
  11. How bad is it? 它有多么坏;
  12. How bad is it for you, crabs? 对你来说有多糟，克拉伯？
  13. a piece of news 一条新闻; 一条消息; 一则新闻; 一则消息; 一条信息;
  14. Today, I read a piece of news in China Daily. 今天，我读了《中国日报》的一则新闻。
  15. gently [ˈdʒentli] adv.温柔地; 温和地; 文静地; 和缓地; 注意点; 小心点; 慢点;
  16. follow-up [ˈfɒləʊ ʌp] adj.重复的; 补充的; 继续的; 接着的;
  17. One man was arrested during the raid and another during a follow-up operation.一人在突袭中被捕，另一人在后续行动中被捕.
  18. kidney [ˈkɪdni] v.肾; 肾脏; (食用的) 动物腰子
  19. liver [ˈlɪvə(r)] n.肝脏; (动物供食用的) 肝;
  20. Liver and kidney are particularly rich in vitamin A.肝脏和肾脏的维生素A含量尤其丰富。
  21. particularly [pəˈtɪkjələli] adv.特别; 尤其
  22. particular [pəˈtɪkjələ] adj.专指的，特指的(与泛指相对); 不寻常的; 格外的; 特别的; 讲究; 挑剔;
  23. I remembered a particular story about a postman who was a murderer.我记得有个故事，讲的是一个邮递员杀人的事情。
  24. slight [slaɪt] adj.轻微的; 略微的; 细小的; 纤细的; 瘦小的; 无须重视的; 不足道的;
  25. Doctors say he has made a slight improvement. 医生说他的病情稍有好转。
  26. minor [ˈmaɪnə(r)] adj. 较小的; 次要的; 轻微的; 小调的; 小音阶的
  27. struggle [ˈstrʌɡl] v.奋斗; 努力; 争取; 艰难地行进; 吃力地进行; 斗争; 抗争;
  28. They had to struggle against all kinds of adversity. 他们不得不同一切困境做斗争。
  29. reconcile [ˈrekənsaɪl] v.使和谐一致; 调和; 使配合; 使和解; 使和好如初; 将就; 妥协;
  30. It’s difficult to reconcile the demands of my job and the desire to be a good father. 我很难做到既兼顾工作，又达成做个好父亲的愿望.
  31. virus(es) [ˈvaɪrəs] n.病毒; 滤过性病毒; 病毒性疾病; 病毒病; (计算机程序中的) 病毒;
  32. There are many different strains of flu virus. 流感病毒有很多不同类型。
  33. scandal [ˈskændl] n.丑行; 使人震惊的丑事; 丑闻; 关于丑行的传言(或报道); 可耻的行为(或态度等); 不可原谅的行为(或态度等);
  34. He loved gossip and scandal. 他喜欢说三道四，散布谣言。
  35. gossip [ˈɡɒsɪp] n.流言蜚语; 闲言碎语; 说长道短; 闲聊; 喜欢传播流言蜚语的人; 爱说长道短(或说三道四)的人;
  36. gossip [ˈɡɒsɪp] v.传播流言蜚语; 说三道四; 说长道短;
  37. He spent the first hour talking gossip.他头一个小时都在闲聊。
  38. raid [reɪd] n.突然袭击; 突击检查; 突然搜查; 抢劫; 打劫;
  39. raid [reɪd] v.突击搜捕; 突然搜查; 突袭; 偷袭; 劫掠; 打劫.
  40. benefit(s) [ˈbenɪfɪts] n.优势; 益处; 成效; 福利费(政府对失业者、病人等提供的补助金); (公司提供的) 褔利，奖金; (保险公司支付的) 给付，保险金;
  41. company benefits [ˈkʌmpəni ˈbenɪfɪts] n.公司福利
  42. downsize [ˈdaʊnsaɪz] v.(公司、企业等) 裁员，精减;
  43. driver’s license

Content of the course

1. Which of the following situations would you most hate to see happen?

2. What’s some bad news you’ve had to tell someone? How did you break it to them? How did they respond?

3. Did you think of these ways to break the bad news? What else might you say?

  • I’m sorry to have to tell you this.
  • I have some good news and some bad news.
  • I’m going to tell this to you straight.
  • I hate to be the one to break this to you.
    In what situations might each of these be most appropriate?

4. Put the words in order to form follow-up questions used to respond to bad news.

  • How did that happen?
  • What am I gonna do?
  • How bad is it?
    Think of the situations from the beginning of the lesson. How would you respond to these pieces of news?

5. Think of three pieces of bad news, e.g. your company is no longer going to pay for your English classes.

  • Student A: Break the bad news to Student B gently.
  • Student B: Respond to Student A, and ask follow-up questions to learn more.
  • Example:
    • I hate to be the one to tell you this, but…
    • That’s terrible…

      6. Today we…

  • discussed ways to break bad news.
  • broke bad news to a colleague.

7. Before you go…

  • Doctor: I have some good news and some bad news.
  • Patient: Give me the good news first.
  • Doctor: Well, they’re going to name a disease after you!
    Now tell your own good news/bad news joke!
