【GL-Advcanced】Dealing with stressful situations - 应对紧张情况

发表于 2020-03-29 更新于 2020-04-02 分类于 EF ， GL ， Advanced

Vocabularies of the course

  1. stress sb out 令(某人)紧张;使(某人)焦虑不安
  2. stress out 压力过大; 经历极大压力; 压力大;
  3. stress n.精神压力; 心理负担; 紧张; 压力; 应力; 强调; 重要性;
  4. stress v.强调; 着重; 重读; 用重音读; (使) 焦虑不安，疲惫不堪;
  5. stressed [strest] adj.焦虑不安; 心力交瘁; 重读的; 受压的; 受应力的;
  6. stressful adj.压力重的; 紧张的
  7. How was the situation deal with? 情况如何处理
  8. boil [bɔɪl] v.(使) 沸腾; 煮沸; 烧开; (把壶、锅等) 里面的水烧开;
  9. boil n.沸腾; 沸点; 疖; 皮下脓肿; 黄水疮
  10. boil over [bɔɪl ˈoʊvər] 沸溢; 潽出; （感情） 爆发; 失去理智; 大怒
  11. explode with anger 勃然大怒; 勃然大怒, 大发脾气
  12. explode [ɪkˈsploʊd] v.爆炸; 爆破; 爆裂; 勃然(大怒); 大发(雷霆); 突然发生(危险); 突然爆发，迸发(感情)
  13. chill out (疲劳、紧张过后） 放松，松弛
  14. calm down (使） 平静下来; （使） 镇定下来; （使） 平定; 平息;
  15. bottle up [ˈbɒtl ʌp] 压抑，抑制（强烈的感情）;
  16. keep emotions inside 保持内心的情感
  17. take time out 暂时停止工作
  18. take a break,relax 休息一下，放松
  19. back off (为避免问题或冲突而） 后退，避让，退缩; 放弃，收回（主张、要求、承诺等）
  20. give someone space 给某人空间
  21. live with 与…同处（一起生活）；与（异性）同居; 学会去适应；接受并忍受
  22. endure [ɪnˈdjʊə(r)] v. 忍耐; 忍受; 持续; 持久
  23. go on about 唠唠叨叨地谈…
  24. talk constantly about 经常谈论
  25. get back at 报复，向…报仇
  26. get revenge against 报复
  27. pick on [pɪk ɒn] 跟…找碴; 故意刁难; 挑中; 选中
  28. treat someone badly 虐待某人
  29. be casual 随便点
  30. bother v.花费时间精力(做某事); 使(某人)烦恼(或担忧、不安); 给(某人)造成麻烦(或痛苦); 打扰; 搭话烦扰

Content of the course

1. What types of situations stress people out?
    - bad traffic
    - being too busy,not enough time in the day
    - hectic lifestyle
    - lost job
    - too many kids

2. Describe a situation that was stressful for you or a friend.Explain what happend. How was the situation deal with?

3. Did you use any of these phrasal verbs?
   Which describe stressful situations?
   Which describe calming advice?
    - boil over
    - bottle up
    - chill out
    - deal with
    - pick on
    - take time out
Stessful situations
    - boil over
    - bottle up
    - pick on

Calming advice
    - deal with
    - chill out
    - take time out

4.Match these phrasal verbs with their definitions
    - boil over   --  explode with anger
    - chill out  -- calm down
    - bottle up - keep emotions inside
    - deal with - manage
    - pick on  - treat someone badly
    - take time out - take a break,relax
    - back off - give someone space
    - live with  -  endure
    - go on about  - talk constantly about
    - get back at  - get revenge against

Which is better - to back off from a stressful situation, to boil over, or to deal with it? Share your opinions with your classmates.

5. There is a stessful situation at your job or school. Talk to a friend about it, and ask for advice. Use phrasal verbs.
    - Student A: Tell Student B about the stessful situation.
    - Student B: Advise Student A about how to handle the situation.

6. Today we ...
    - talked about stessful situations.
    - used some phrasal verbs related to stress.
    - Gave advice about dealing with stress.

7.Before you go ...
    - Why is stress management importmant in your life?
    - Have you tried any interesting or unusual ways to relieve stess?
