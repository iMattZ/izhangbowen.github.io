【GL-Advcanced】Talking about education - 谈论教育

发表于 2020-03-28 更新于 2020-04-02 分类于 EF ， GL

Vocabularies of the course

  1. in one’s first few years 头几年
  2. childhood educaiton 早期教育; 儿童教育; 学前教育; 童年教育; 幼儿教育
  3. Describe what wasn’t good 描述什么不好
  4. talent [ˈtælənt] n.天才; 天资; 天赋; 有才能的人; 人才; (统称) 性感的人;
  5. creativity [ˌkriːeɪˈtɪvəti] n.创造力；创造性;
  6. serious subjects 严肃的话题
  7. the arts 艺术; 艺术活动; 艺术学院; 文科; 艺术类;
  8. Valued v.重视; 珍视; 给…估价; 给…定价;
  9. Stigmatized [ˈstɪɡmətaɪzd] v.使感到羞耻; 侮蔑
  10. innovation n.(新事物、思想或方法的) 创造; 创新; 改革; 新思想; 新方法
  11. was highly valued 被高度重视
  12. Non-standard accents 非标准口音
  13. got awarded for 获得奖励
  14. squandered [ˈskwɒndəd] v.浪费，挥霍(金钱、时间等)
  15. be squandered 挥霍
  16. needlessly adv.不需要地，不必要地;
  17. Money was squandered needlessly 钱被不必要地挥霍了
  18. are reflecting on 正在反思
  19. Did your school value creativity? 你的学校重视创造力吗
  20. value v.重视; 珍视; 给…估价; 给…定价;
  21. somewhat adv.有点; 有几分; 稍微
  22. aspect [ˈæspekt] n.方面; 层面; 样子; 外观; 外表; 朝向; 方位

Content of the course

1. Name five things you learned in your first few years at school. You have one minute.

2. Try to remember your childhood education. Describe what was good about it. Describe what wasn't good.

What would you change about your childhood education?
3. Which of these words did you use? 
    - sdfd
    - talent
    - creativity
    - serious subjects
    - the arts
    * Which were valued and which were stigmatized in your childhood education?
    - Valued
    - Stigmatized
    Try to add more words to the lists.

4.Look at the statements below. Which are true about your childhood education? Which are false?
    Statement                                true/false
    Innovation was highly valued.
    Non-standard accents were stigmatized.
    Teachers encouraged my sport talent.
    Students got awarded for agreeing with the teacher.
    Money was squandered needlessly.

5.You and a friend are reflecting on your childhood education.
    Student A: Ask Student B questions about what was valued and what was stigmatized in his or her childhood education.
    Student B: Express your opinions about childhood education.
    - Did your school value creativity?
    - Somewhat. We had great classes in the arts, but I would say the focus was on the serious subjects.

6.Today we...
    - Recalled what we learned during our first years in school.
    - Expressed our opinions regarding what was good or bad about our childhood education.
    - Disscussed which aspects of our education were valued and which were stigmatized.

7.Before you go...
    - What was valued and stigmatized in your parents' or children's education? How is that different from your education?
    - What do you enjoy most about online classes? What is the most challenging aspect of learning online?
