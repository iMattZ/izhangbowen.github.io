Content of the course

1. In what ways do you give back to your community?

2. What are some characteristics of a charitable person?

Who is the most charitable person you know?

3. Did you use any of these words when describing a charitable person? What other words can you think of?

  • altruistic —- self interested
  • civic-minded —– self centered
  • compassionate —– heartless
  • humanitarian —— selfish
  • philanthropic —– mean/misanthropic
  • duty
  • get off the fence 离开围栏
    What are the opposites of these words?

4. Fill in the blanks with the appropriate word.

    1. If you think of other people’s needs before your own, then you are ___.(selfless)
    1. If you do something because you feel it is right, then you do it out of a sense of ___.(duty)
    1. A person who is concerned with improving people’s lives - for example, by improving their living conditions or education - is said to be a ___.(humanitarian)
    1. If you giving more time or money than is expected, then you are described as ___.(generous)
    1. A _ person feels sympathy for people who are suffering, whereas an _ person does not care about other people’s needs or feelings.(compassionate,inconsiderate)
    1. If you have a strong sense of __, then you feel the need to take care of the people in your local community. Such a person is described as __.(civic responsibility, civic-minded)
    1. A _ person is kind and helpful, whereas a _ person thinks only of himself. The latter may be called an ___.(good-hearted, selfish, egotist)

5. You are planning a charity fund raiser next month. Look at the list of volunteers.

  • Student A: Work with Student B to choose one person to join the planning committee. Be sure to explain why the candidates are suitable or not suitable.
  • Student B: Work with Student A to choose the best candidate.
  • Pablo is particularly compassionate, but he’s uncomfortable around people he doesn’t know well.
  • Lily is extremely philanthropic, but this is her first time ever to volunteer.
  • Dave has always been generous, but sometimes he can be a bit pushy.
