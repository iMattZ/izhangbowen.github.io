【GL-Advcanced】Holiday destinations - 度假胜地

发表于 2020-05-08 分类于 EF ， GL ， Advanced

您是否有朋友前来要求您向他推荐度假胜地？ 学会如何给出一个具有说服力的建议。

vocabulary of the course

  1. destination(s) [ˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃənz] n.目的地; 终点
  2. convince [kənˈvɪns] v.使确信; 使相信; 使信服; 说服，劝说(某人做某事);
  3. tourist(s) [ˈtʊərɪsts] n.旅游者; 观光者; 游客; (在国外参加) 巡回比赛的运动队队员;
  4. dramatically [drəˈmætɪk(ə)li] adv.戏剧地; 显著地
  5. steadily [stedɪlɪ] adv.稳定地; 坚定地; 坚固地; 不断地
  6. slightly [ˈslaɪtli] adv.略微; 稍微; 身材瘦小的;
  7. tourism [ˈtʊərɪzəm] n.旅游业; 观光业;
  8. rainforest [ˈreɪnfɒrɪst] n.(热带) 雨林
  9. the Amazon rainforest 亚马逊雨林
  10. exotic [ɪɡˈzɒtɪk] adj.来自异国(尤指热带国家)的; 奇异的; 异国情调的; 异国风味的;
  11. tropical [ˈtrɒpɪkl] adj.热带的; 来自热带的; 产于热带的
  12. vegetation [ˌvedʒəˈteɪʃn] n.(统称) 植物; (尤指某地或环境的) 植被，植物群落，草木
  13. the area of tropical vegetation 热带植被区
  14. wildlife [ˈwaɪldlaɪf] n.野生动物; 野生生物
  15. wildlife falls dramatically 野生动物数量急剧下降
  16. tourist numbers 旅游人数
  17. the fragile ecosystem 脆弱的生态系统
  18. fragile [ˈfrædʒaɪl] adj.易碎的; 易损的; 不牢固的; 脆弱的; 纤巧的; 精细的; 纤巧美丽的;
  19. ecosystem [ˈiːkəʊsɪstəm] n. 生态系统
  20. environmental [ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmentl] adj.自然环境的; 生态环境的; 有关环境的; (影响个体或事物行为或发展的) 环境的;
  21. awareness [əˈweənəs] n.知道; 认识; 意识; 兴趣;

Content of the course

1. What are the most famous tourist destinations?

2. Convince the class they should visit your city! Give as many reasons as you can.

  • Numbers of tourists
  • Spring
  • Summer
  • Autumn
  • Winter
    Is the number of tourists coming to your city affected by the seasons?

    3. Which of these words did you use? Put them into categories.

  • verbs
  • adverbs
  • increase
  • decrease
  • dramatically
  • steadily
  • slightly
    How do you think tourism has increased or decreased in the world over the last thirty years?

    4. Complete the blanks with the discourse markers below.

    However , In addition , First of all , In the end , Furthermore
    First of all, the Amazon rainforest is the largest and most exotic forest in the world. However, the area of tropical vegetation and wildlife falls dramatically every year. In addition, tourist numbers and pollution rise steadily. Furthemore, the July summer holidays bring a huge increase in tourists, who cause damage to the fragile ecosystem.
    In the end, we must find a way for tourism numbers to remain steady and environmental awareness to rise dramatically.

5. You have been asked to give a presentation about trends in tourism in your country in different seasons.

Describe how tourism is affected by seasonal shifts.

  • Spring
  • Summer
  • Autumn
  • Winter

6. Today we…

  • described travel trends
  • practiced using discourse markers such as however, furthermore, in addition and in the end.
  • recommended tourist destinations.

7. Before you go…

  • What is the best tourist destination you’ve ever visited? What was the worst? Why ?
  • What do you predict for the future of tourism in your country?
